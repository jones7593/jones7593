CCSF Student Housing Wait List

Fall Semester 2023

Housing for continuing students is available in the form of a random lottery process during the middle of the summer semester.

The continuing student housing application will open on July 15, 2023.










   

Student Housing Guidelines

First year students have priority.

Two thirds of our first year students will receive accomodations.

Upper class students are not guaranteed housing, due to limited space.

It is important for students to take housing limitations into account when planning their college experience. It is important for students to recognize that they are likely to begin living off-campus as early as their second year.
